Aesthetic boundary

One contract. Many faces.

  • Anatomy in pattern packs

    Pattern packs declare slots and variant axes. They consume tokens; they never declare values.

  • Values in aesthetic packs

    Aesthetic packs override token values. Color, type, motion, radius — every visible decision lives here.
